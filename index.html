MARKET OVERVIEW

The Philippines Data Center Market was valued at USD 298 million in 2021 and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% to reach USD 635 million by 2027. The country is witnessing increased hyperscale investments, with the government easing FDI regulations. Due to the reduction of taxes from 30% to 25% by the Philippine government, the data center market in the Philippines is expected to grow during the forecast period.

The COVID-19 outbreak and a complete lockdown to contain the spread of the infection significantly accelerated the adoption of cloud services in the country. Stringent mobility restrictions during the lockdown in the country compelled most businesses to shift a part of their workloads to the cloud platform from on-premise facilities, ensuring efficient and scalable operations.

Factors driving the Philippines data center market include the increasing gaming population, a booming Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) industry, and the increasing popularity of Content Delivery Network (CDN) and online streaming.

Manila is one of the most preferred locations for data center operators, followed by Mindanao and Cebu.
